# Launch Blueprint

## Positioning

Launch as an English-only tool for extracting tables from text-based PDFs to XLSX and CSV.

Primary message:

> Extract tables from PDF to Excel online. Free, private, no signup.

Do not promise scanned PDF/OCR support in v1. Present OCR as a waitlist/interest signal.

## Weekly review

Track:

- Indexed pages
- Search impressions and CTR
- Upload starts
- Extraction successes and failures
- XLSX downloads
- CSV downloads
- OCR interest clicks
- Top failure messages

Use this data to decide whether v2 should add OCR, bank statement templates, or batch conversion.
