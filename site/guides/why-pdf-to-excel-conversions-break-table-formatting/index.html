Why PDF to Excel Conversions Break Table Formatting

PDFs store visual instructions, not spreadsheet logic. That is why rows, columns, and merged cells can break during conversion.

Common causes

  • Columns are aligned visually but not encoded as cells.
  • Headers span multiple columns.
  • Tables continue across page breaks.
  • Scanned pages have no text layer.

How to improve results

Use text-based PDFs, avoid low-quality scans, and verify totals after conversion. For scanned documents, wait for OCR support or use a dedicated OCR tool first.
