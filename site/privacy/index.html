Privacy Policy

This tool is designed to minimize stored data. Uploaded PDFs are processed temporarily for the current conversion request and are deleted after processing.

Files

We do not provide accounts, saved history, or persistent file storage. The backend writes uploads to temporary storage only while extracting tables.

Analytics

The site may use privacy-conscious analytics and product events to understand usage, conversion success, and failure rates.

Scanned PDFs

Image-based or scanned PDFs need OCR, which will be supported later. This policy will be updated before OCR processing is added.
